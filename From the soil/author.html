Hi, I'm Elisa Ana the Voice Behind "From the Soil"

I’m 15 years old, and I come from both Kikuyu and Mozambican roots. Growing up with this blend of cultures has shaped the way I see the world — full of color, complexity, and strength. Through From the Soil, I share stories, thoughts, and reflections rooted in my identity, my journey, and the hopes I carry as a young African voice.

This blog is my soil — and every post is a seed I hope takes root in someone’s heart.
